About the Game

Henry is an auditory memory game, first released by Tiger Electronics in 1996 as a handheld game console. Henry is designed to challenge your memorization abilities with seventy-five unique sounds.

Press Play to shuffle songs and 16 sounds will load onto the game board. Press the Henry button to play a sound and attempt to match it to a cell on the grid. You win by matching four sounds in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. It's harder than it looks!

  1. Press the glowing Henry button to play a sound
  2. Match the sound on the grid
  3. Find four in a line
  4. Press reset to start a new game
